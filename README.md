# meta_principles

Cross-project development principles — the durable rules and bets worth carrying
from one project to the next. This repo is the **canonical home** for principles
that are good development practice across multiple individual projects.

See **[PRINCIPLES.md](PRINCIPLES.md)** for the full set, grouped by theme:

1. Goals & mindset
2. Architecture
3. Source of truth, derived artifacts & determinism
4. Dependencies
5. Security, secrets & privacy
6. Testing & quality
7. Failure modes & observability
8. Documentation & knowledge transfer
9. Process & change discipline
10. Working with Claude (LLM-using projects)
11. Managing Claude skills across projects
12. The 15-decision brainstorm (answer before any build)

— plus a terminology-conventions table.

## Scope

- **Belongs here:** principles that generalize across projects.
- **Belongs in a project, not here:** project-specific decisions
  (`DECISIONS_LOG.md`, MADR format) and operational Claude skills
  (`/brainstorm`, `/mvp-build`).

New cross-project principles get logged here, not in a per-machine local file.
